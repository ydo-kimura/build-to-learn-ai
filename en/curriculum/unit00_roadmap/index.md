# Unit 0: Overall Roadmap and Guidelines

Welcome! This repository is a comprehensive curriculum designed to take you from the absolute basics of machine learning to the cutting edge of LLM application development and Autonomous AI Agents.

This curriculum is structured as a collection of "micro-PoCs" (Proof of Concept) designed to be completed in a few hours. By dedicating time to code and learn daily, you will build robust skills and absolute confidence as a professional AI Engineer.

## 📝 How to Use this Curriculum

Each unit is structured under a unified pedagogy consisting of 4 steps:

1. **Explanation**: Thoroughly master the conceptual background, mathematical intuition, and business trade-offs of the technology.
2. **Implementation Example**: Study complete, working Python implementations with detailed step-by-step documentation.
3. **Practice (Assignment)**: Solve a hands-on, self-directed business assignment using a separate dataset. You choose the models and parameters and justify your choices!
4. **Answer Key**: Compare your solution with a professional architect's trade-off analysis and reference implementation.
